<p>Outstanding student leaders are recognised for their contribution and
service to the school during Annual Prize Presentation Ceremony. This serves
as an opportunity to acknowledge their hard work, leadership, and the positive
impact they have made on the school community. It also inspires their peers
to strive for excellence and take on leadership roles with a sense of purpose
and commitment.
