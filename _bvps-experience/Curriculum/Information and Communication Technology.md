<h4><strong>IT Skills Training for All Pupils</strong></h4>
<p>In Bukit View Primary School, we want to prepare our students to be a
future ready and responsible digital learner. To achieve that, we have
1:1 computing device for every student to learn to use ICT during their
lessons. Every student is given the opportunity to work individually or
collaborate with their peers on class work / project work. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Cyber Wellness also plays an important part in our ICT lessons. It helps
to equip our students with the knowledge to look into their positive well-being
as they navigate the cyberspace.</p>
