The first component of the Bukit View Learn for Life programme is its Applied Learning Programme (ALP) ---- Inspiring Reflective and Innovative Self-directed learner (IRIS). Aligned to the first student outcome of the school vision, IRIS aims to develop students to be Self-Directed Learners who have strong foundations, are motivated in learning, and being reflective and Innovative.
