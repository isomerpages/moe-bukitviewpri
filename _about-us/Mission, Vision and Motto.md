<h3><strong>Graciousness</strong></h3>
We care and help others, are considerate, and greet and communicate with
everyone politely.<p></p>
<h4><strong>Responsibility</strong></h4>
We will do our duties, take ownership of our own safety and belongings,
exercise self-control and be curious and reflective as we work hard towards
excellence.<p></p>
<h4><strong>Respect</strong></h4>
We are kind in our actions and words, treat everyone and their properties
with respect, and have empathy for others by listening actively and appreciating
their perspectives.<p></p>
<h4><strong>Integrity</strong></h4>
We deliver on our promises, are truthful, and choose to do what is right
even when no one is watching.<p></p>
<h4><strong>Tenacity</strong></h4>
We are courageous, adaptive, and determined not to give up, even when
faced with difficulties.<p></p>
