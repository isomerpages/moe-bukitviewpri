<h3>SCHOOL CREST</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/About%20Us/school%20logo_2160%20x1080.jpg">

<p>The logo, incorporating the initials of the school 'BVPS', symbolises
a child with outstretched hands reaching for excellence.</p>
<p>The circle symbolises a well-rounded education and emphasises the aim
of the school in providing an all-round development - moral, intellectual,
physical, aesthetics, social and emotional - for its pupils.</p>
<p><strong>Sky Blue</strong>&nbsp;depicts ultimate success in achieving desired
goals.</p>
<p><strong>Navy Blue</strong>&nbsp;shows a happy balance in all aspects of
education.</p>
<p><strong>White </strong>represents virtue and integrity.</p>
