<h4><strong>Our School Values&nbsp;</strong></h4>
<strong>&nbsp;</strong>The school’s character education is premised on
the school values of GR<sup>2</sup>IT: Graciousness, Responsibility, Respect,&nbsp;Integrity&nbsp;and
Tenacity.&nbsp;As Bukit&nbsp;Viewans, our students are guided by these
values in their thoughts,&nbsp;words&nbsp;and actions.&nbsp;
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 200%" height="auto" width="200%" alt="" src="/images/About Us/GR2IT_values.png">
</div>
<h4><strong>Graciousness</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
Greet&nbsp;and&nbsp;communicate&nbsp;with everyone politely&nbsp;
</li>
<li>Are caring and will help others&nbsp;
</li>
<li>Are considerate, are mindful of how their words and actions affect&nbsp;others,
and&nbsp;will give way to&nbsp;others&nbsp;
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Responsibility</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Are&nbsp;responsible for&nbsp;their safety and their&nbsp;belongings,
and&nbsp;will exercise self-control&nbsp;
</p></li>
<li> Are&nbsp;responsible members of the class,&nbsp;family&nbsp;and society
who will do their duties and contribute positively to others&nbsp;
</li>
<li>Are&nbsp;curious and reflective in their learning and will work hard to
strive towards excellence
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Respect</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
Treat themselves and everyone with respect, value others and are kind
in their words and actions toward&nbsp;others&nbsp;
</li>
<li>Show respect for the property of others and the environment and will not
cause harm to them&nbsp;
</li>
<li> Have empathy and are sensitive to how others feel by listening actively
and appreciating their perspectives&nbsp;
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Integrity</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
Tell the truth and admit their mistakes&nbsp;
</li>
<li>Choose to do&nbsp;what is right even when no one is watching&nbsp;
</li>
<li>Will follow through on their words and deliver on their promises&nbsp;
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Tenacity</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
Remain positive,&nbsp;persevere&nbsp;and do not give up, even when faced
with difficulties&nbsp;
</li>
<li>Are&nbsp;courageous to take on challenges and view them as opportunities
for growth&nbsp;
</li>
<li>Adapt to situations, reflect on setbacks, innovate and try different methods
to tackle problems and challenges&nbsp;
</li>
</ul>
